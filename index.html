About Me

Hey, my name is Dan. I am a soon-to-be graduate of UW- Eau Claire with a major in Communication Studies and minor in Multimedia Communication. Once I graduate, I hope to find a job in sports media. Over the past four plus years, I have competed for my university’s cross country, indoor track, and outdoor track teams. Some other interests of mine include collecting vinyl and CDs, watching TV shows and films of all sorts of genres, and occasionally seeing what I can concoct in the kitchen.
